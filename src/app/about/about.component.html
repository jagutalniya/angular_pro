About Talniya Student Management System

Our Student Management System is designed to simplify and digitize school operations — from multiple school handling by Super Admin to class management by teachers.

  • 🧑‍💼 Super Admin can manage multiple schools independently.
  • 🏫 Each School Admin can manage their own teachers and students.
  • 👨‍🏫 Teachers can view and manage students of their assigned classes only.
  • 🎓 Students can view their profile, academic info, and class details.

This system was created with the vision to empower rural and semi-urban schools digitally — with the first example being Talniya Public School, Degana.
